WESTEROS MULTISERVICE COMPANY

Westeros Multiservice Company was born out of the passion to provide solutions to different security needs in the society. Founded in year 2019, it has undergone different phases of refinement to become a leading voice in one of the emerging tech markets, the Extra Low Voltage (ELV) Systems. We strive to make various security challenges posing home owners, offices and industrial players a thing of the past, by providing adequate and well thought out solutions. These solutions are tailored in such a way that, though generic, it is also customer-centered. We want to help businesses and home owners maximize profit and reap economic value.

WHAT WE OFFER

We help homes and businesses better control and protect their properties by providing solutions that cut across several Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM's) who provide integrated services with Internet of Things (IoT) enabled devices. We help facilitate the consulting, design, implementation or installation aspects, with client’s budget in view.

We also offer you value for money by providing you a one stop online market place to purchase top category OEM devices for home and business automation. We pick variety of devices from top tier manufactures like Schneider, ABB, Siemens, KNX etc., at very unbeatable prices. We provide options to request for electronic proforma invoices or quote, home/office delivery with due dates.

WESTESERVE - A SUBSIDIARY OF WESTEROS MULTISERVICE COMPANY

Westeserve is a technology infrastructure provider. We enable homes and businesses connect devices that enhance security, way of life and functionality. At the heart of the Westeserve is automation, we automate homes for seamless life style and we are keen on encouraging well thought out solutions that are locally feasible and globally adaptable. Our services range from providing an online market place for affordable home security and automation devices, providing guide lines on how to install or improve upon existing home automation systems, provision of after sales service and maintenance of home security and automation devices.

The online presence of Westeserve is where customers can find out more about the company. The interactive platform which is created to enable users place orders, choose means of delivery, pay for items and have them shipped to their destination. Our market page includes detailed list of smart sensory devices, which ranges from Visual (smart cameras), Sound, Proximity, Temperature, Pressure, and Level Detectors. What we offer is a low-cost way of shopping for materials that can be used during system integration of home and industrial appliances. We want to help our prospective clients make informed decisions for their security and automation needs and also provide Installation, Commissioning and Maintenance services for products bought from our platform at unbeatable prices.
